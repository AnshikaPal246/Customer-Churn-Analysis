# Customer-Churn-Analysis
Analyzed telecom customer churn using Python, SQL, and Power BI to identify key drivers of customer attrition. Built an interactive dashboard showcasing churn trends across customer segments, contracts, payment methods, and tenure. Generated actionable retention strategies to improve customer loyalty and reduce churn

## Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing customer churn behavior in a telecom company to identify factors that contribute to customer attrition and provide actionable business recommendations.
## Objective
The primary objective is to understand why customers leave the company and determine the key drivers behind churn.
## Tools & Technologies
* Python (Pandas, NumPy)
* SQL
* Power BI
## Project Workflow
1. Data Cleaning and Preprocessing
2. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
3. SQL-Based Business Analysis
4. KPI Development
5. Dashboard Design in Power BI
6. Business Insights and Recommendations
## Key Findings
* Month-to-month contract customers exhibited the highest churn rate.
* Customers with higher monthly charges were more likely to leave.
* Customers with shorter tenure had significantly higher churn.
* Electronic check users showed the highest churn among payment methods.
* Customers without tech support demonstrated higher churn rates.
* Fiber optic customers experienced higher churn compared to other internet service types.
## Business Recommendations
* Introduce incentives for long-term contracts.
* Improve onboarding and retention programs for new customers.
* Enhance customer support services.
* Review pricing strategies for high-risk customer segments.
* Promote automatic payment methods to improve customer retention.
